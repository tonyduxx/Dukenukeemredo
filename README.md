# Hydra Heros

A modern remake of Duke Nukem 3D inspired shooter game built with HTML5 Canvas and JavaScript. Choose your hero and battle waves of enemies in this action-packed top-down shooter!

## Features

### Character Selection
- **DUXX**: The agile scout with enhanced speed and precision weapons
  - Speed: ★★★★☆
  - Health: ★★★☆☆
  - Damage: ★★★☆☆
  - Fast movement, moderate health, balanced damage

- **VAN**: The balanced warrior with versatile combat abilities
  - Speed: ★★★☆☆
  - Health: ★★★★☆
  - Damage: ★★★★☆
  - Well-rounded stats for all-around performance

- **GRIZZ**: The heavy tank with maximum health and devastating firepower
  - Speed: ★★☆☆☆
  - Health: ★★★★★
  - Damage: ★★★★★
  - Slow but powerful, high health and damage

### Gameplay Features
- **Top-down shooter** with mouse-aimed shooting
- **Dynamic enemy spawning** from all sides
- **Particle effects** for muzzle flashes and impacts
- **Health and ammo management** with reload mechanics
- **Score system** for competitive play
- **Responsive controls** with keyboard and mouse support

## Controls

### Character Selection
- **Mouse**: Click to select character
- **Enter**: Start game (after character selection)

### In-Game Controls
- **WASD** or **Arrow Keys**: Move player
- **Mouse**: Aim and look around
- **Left Click**: Shoot
- **R**: Reload weapon
- **Mouse Movement**: Control aiming direction

## How to Play

1. **Select Your Hero**: Choose from DUXX, VAN, or GRIZZ based on your preferred playstyle
2. **Survive**: Fight off waves of enemies that spawn from the edges of the screen
3. **Score Points**: Defeat enemies to earn points
4. **Manage Resources**: Keep track of your health and ammo
5. **Reload**: Press R to reload when you run out of ammo
6. **Survive as Long as Possible**: The game gets progressively harder as more enemies spawn

## Game Mechanics

### Character Stats
Each character has unique stats that affect gameplay:
- **Health**: How much damage you can take
- **Speed**: How fast you can move
- **Damage**: How much damage your shots deal
- **Ammo Capacity**: How many shots you can fire before reloading
- **Reload Time**: How long it takes to reload

### Enemy Behavior
- Enemies spawn from the edges of the screen
- They move directly toward the player
- Each enemy has health and can be damaged
- Enemies deal damage on contact
- Defeating enemies earns points

### Visual Effects
- **Muzzle Flash**: Particles appear when shooting
- **Impact Effects**: Red particles when bullets hit enemies
- **Health Bars**: Visual representation of enemy health
- **Crosshair**: Aiming reticle in the center of the screen
